# Sergio Vision — Design System

## Aesthetic: "Amstel Bay Executive Noir" (unchanged from scaffold)

- **Palette:** Obsidian `#0B0C10` (bg), Deep Charcoal `#1F2833` (cards), Oranje `#FF6A00` (accent), Amber `#FF9E00` (secondary), Off-White `#C5C6C7` (text)
- **Typography:** Outfit (sans headings), Playfair Display italic (drama/accents), JetBrains Mono (data/labels), Space Grotesk (body)
- **Noise overlay:** Inline SVG `feTurbulence` at 0.05 opacity
- **Radius:** `rounded-[2rem]` to `rounded-[3rem]` system
- **Buttons:** `scale(1.03)` magnetic hover, sliding bg layer
- **GSAP:** `power3.out` entrances, `power2.inOut` morphs, stagger 0.08 text / 0.15 cards

## Client brand

Brand DNA: **Tillid · Kvalitet · Premium**  
Shop: Glasurit (BASF premium), 3-year warranty, SKAD member, Fordels-værksted for Tryg/Alka/TJM/If/Topdanmark, DTC racing specialty.
